Instructions

In this experiment, you're pretending to be diving for oysters. Each oyster has a pearl inside that has a certain value. Your goal is to choose the oysters with the higher-valued pearls.

You'll be trained to predict the value of the pearl inside an oyster by the pattern on its shell. Different oysters have different shell patterns. The value of the pearl inside varies according to the oyster.

We'll train you by presenting examples of oyster shell patterns. For each pair of oysters you see, your job will be to choose the oyster you think has a higher valued pearl inside.

You will press the left arrow key (←) to select the oyster on the left, and the right arrow key (→) to select the oyster on the right.

Training will end when you demonstrate that you can consistently choose the higher-valued oyster. After training, there will be an opportunity to earn real bonus money, depending on how well you can choose the higher-valued oyster.
